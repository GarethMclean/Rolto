# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

Rolto ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information**: Such as your name, email address, and company name when you sign up, contact us, or use Rolto.
- **Chat Data**: Conversations and metadata generated through your use of the Rolto chatbot.
- **Usage Data**: Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device details.
- **Cookies**: We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve performance and analytics.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Operate and improve the Rolto platform.
- Respond to inquiries and provide support.
- Monitor usage and ensure platform security.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell or rent your personal data.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery). These partners are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service, fulfill legal obligations, or as required for support and operational purposes.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access or correct your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Withdraw consent at any time

To make a request, contact us at [contact@rolto.io](mailto:contact@rolto.io).

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Rolto is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in some regions). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
